Good fundraising practices

  • Foster a sense of community and involvement among donors by sharing stories, updates, and milestones related to your fundraising campaigns
  • Show appreciation for every donation received, recognizing the invaluable support of your donors and volunteers
  • Keep donors informed about the progress of your fundraising efforts and the impact of their contributions, fostering trust and accountability
  • Adhere to legal and ethical standards in all fundraising activities, ensuring transparency, accountability, and donor trust

What makes it a good investment?

  • Define what sets your project apart from competitors and why it's uniquely positioned to succeed. Emphasize any innovative features, technologies, or approaches that differentiate your offering
  • Outline your business model and revenue strategy, explaining how your project generates value and sustains profitability over the long term. Provide clarity on pricing, distribution channels, and monetization strategies
  • Acknowledge and address potential risks associated with the project, along with mitigation strategies. Transparency about risks demonstrates foresight and builds investor confidence
  • Clearly outline the benefits and incentives for investors, such as equity ownership, profit-sharing arrangements, or exclusive perks. Highlight what investors stand to gain from supporting your project financially
